Top Picks

1. Interstellar

Director: Christopher Nolan

A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival. It is a visual masterpiece unlike anything else.

Why I like it (Click to expand)

The soundtrack by Hans Zimmer is incredible, and the depiction of black holes is scientifically accurate.

2. The Matrix

Director: The Wachowskis

A computer hacker learns from mysterious rebels about the true nature of his reality and his role in the war against its controllers. The action scenes defined a generation.

Why I like it (Click to expand)

It completely changed how sci-fi movies were made. The concept of "The One" is executed perfectly.
