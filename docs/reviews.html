Dune: Part Two

This movie was an epic continuation of the saga. The cinematography is absolutely stunning. However, the pacing in the middle felt slightly slow.

The final duel between Paul and Feyd-Rautha was intense, but I was surprised how abruptly the movie ended after he took the throne.

The Batman

A dark, gritty take on the detective side of the character. Robert Pattinson brings a brooding intensity that fits the role perfectly.

The reveal of the Riddler's plan to flood the city was a great twist that raised the stakes perfectly.

Oppenheimer

A haunting biopic that feels like a horror movie at times. The sound design is incredible.

The ending scene with Einstein, revealing that they may have actually started a chain reaction that destroys the world, was chilling.

Unexpectedly deep and philosophical while still being hilarious. Ryan Gosling steals the show as Ken.
